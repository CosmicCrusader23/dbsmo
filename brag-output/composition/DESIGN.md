# DBSMO Brag Design Notes

- Tone: fake Series A launch from 2016, mapped to yc-parody.
- Canvas: 1920x1080, 21 seconds.
- Colors from source app: #f2f2f7 background, #ffffff cards, #222233 strong text, #444455 body text, #33aaff cyan, #ff66aa pink, #8866cc purple.
- Typeface: Inter/system sans, matching the Next app.
- Visuals: product UI recreation, not abstract motion. Use dashboard cards, practice form, answer preview, progress ring, topic bars, and cohort table.
- Motion: serious deck pacing, hard cuts, restrained reveals, readable holds.
- Audio: low corporate bed plus sparse clicks/soft impacts.
