# Brag Plan: DBSMO Training

## What is this app?

DBSMO Training is a self-paced Diocesan Boys' School mathematics olympiad platform where students choose a topic, solve real problems with live math previews, receive automatic grading, and track steady improvement.

## The angle

Treat the product like a piece of athletic equipment for mathematical thinking: precise, immediate, and satisfying to use. The film moves from a disciplined logo trace into a cascade of real product states—dashboard, topic choice, live answer preview, and correct/streak feedback—so the promise of momentum is demonstrated, not merely claimed.

## Hook (first 2-3 seconds)

A cyan math curve and the MO monogram trace themselves out of a deep navy panel. The phrase “Olympiad math. With momentum.” arrives as a sharp, calm payoff rather than a loud slogan.

## Key moments (the middle)

- The actual `training dashboard` assembles around a progress ring and the app's `Best average` metric.
- `Choose your focus` reveals topic cards sequentially, including the app's distinctive `Endless` mode.
- Typed math becomes a live rendered fraction, then resolves into the real `Correct. Streak 4.` feedback state.

## Outro / punchline

The formula resolves into the electric-blue MO mark, followed by `DBSMO TRAINING` and the concise line `Pick a topic. Keep going.`

## User flow worth showing

Dashboard progress → choose a practice category → type an answer and see the KaTeX preview → receive automatic correct/streak feedback.

## Tone

- Preset: app-store
- Creative direction: cool kinetic motion in the language of a modern product advertisement
- Interpretation: Premium product-film pacing with crisp, camera-like moves, card choreography, and hard readable holds; energetic without caps-lock chaos.

## Format: landscape — 1920x1080

## Duration: 22.00 seconds

## Visual identity (from the project)

- Background: `#f2f2f7`
- Accent: `#33aaff` (with app-native `#ff66aa`, `#8866cc`, and `#77bb00` for UI states)
- Text: `#222233`
- Display font: Archivo Black for ad-scale statements; the source UI's Inter for recreated product panels
- Body font: Inter; JetBrains Mono for timing/data chrome
- Strongest visual element: the dashboard's conic progress ring, floating best-average chip, and the live math answer preview

## Share copy (draft)

DBSMO Training turns every problem into a next move: choose a focus, solve with live math preview, and keep the streak going.

## Audio direction

- Role: upbeat, clean modern-product music bed with sparse interaction accents
- Music: `happy-beats-business-moves-vol-10-by-ende-dot-app.mp3` (109.96 BPM, compact and punchy)
- Music treatment: start at a low confident level, support visual hits rather than dominate them, then fade under the final lockup
- Music cue guidance: bundled preset at `assets/music/cues/happy-beats-business-moves-vol-10-by-ende-dot-app.music-cues.json`; major locks at 15.82s (correct/streak), 20.19s (logo landing), and 21.83s (final copy); dashboard cards use beats 4.10/5.19/6.28 and category cards use 8.22/9.29/10.38
- Audio-reactive treatment: subtle; low-band energy gently breathes the dashboard halo and the final logo aura. No waveform, equalizer, strobing, or text pulsing.
- SFX posture: moderate and motion-matched; a low-risk click on submit, soft impacts on major scene locks, and one warm logo payoff
- Audio-coupled moments: formula type, submit click, correct state, progress-ring completion, logo landing
- Restraint rule: no continuous bright ticks, no sound on every card, and no SFX that competes with readable text.
